# Product Vision

BabyCare Companion should feel calm, dependable, and family-owned: a small system that makes important dates easier to manage without pretending to replace professional care.

## Principles

- **Family first:** reduce administrative load, not add it.
- **Privacy by design:** use local JSON, ICS, Markdown, and optional local backups.
- **Reliability over novelty:** no silent changes; every write is logged and idempotent.
- **Clinician-confirmed health data:** reminders represent approved plans, not medical recommendations.
- **Portable core:** calendar providers are adapters around shared data and rules.

## Roadmap

| Version | Focus |
| --- | --- |
